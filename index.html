About Me

  • Education: I am a second-year undergraduate in Mathematics at the School of Mathematical Sciences, University of Science and Technology of China. (PB2401)
  • Experience: I was born in Changsha and raised in Guilin. Growing up with parents who are both mathematics teachers, my exposure to the subject started early and naturally. During high school, I spent time self-studying for mathematics olympiads. I earned a few minor awards — though they offered little practical advantage for university admissions. Still, this experience gave me the opportunity to visit several cities: Hangzhou, Shanghai, Ningbo, Wenzhou, Chongqing… After College Entrance Examination, I got admitted to USTC and I currently live in Hefei. Ultimately, guided by a simple and enduring interest, I chose to pursue mathematics as my major.
  • Interests: My interest mainly lies in Partial Differential Equations in applied mathematics. I am also interested in the application of Artificial Intelligence to mathematical research.
  • Language: Chinese (native), English (fluent), French (basic).
  • Honors: Excellent Student Scholarship (silver, 2025), Freshman Scholarship (2024).
